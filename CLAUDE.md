## Deployment Rule

Vercel deploys from `main`. If you are working on a worktree branch or any branch that is not `main`, you MUST merge to `main` and push after every commit. Do not leave work stranded on a feature branch. The sequence is:

1. Commit on your branch
2. Push your branch
3. Merge your branch into `main` (resolve conflicts if needed)
4. Push `main`

Never tell Hannah "it's on the branch" — she expects to see it live at montessorimakersgroup.org within minutes of you saying it's done.
